Why Not Self-Publish?

These days it's really straightforward to publish on markets like Amazon. If you're looking for wider distribution or have specific concerns about publishing and registering titles under your own legal name, we can help.

1

ISBN Procurement & Registration

Not only are the current prices for ISBNS extortionate, they also require a tranche of personally identifable information during the registration process. We can file on your behalf, keeping your personal details and anonymity secure without spending a dime.

2

Expanded Distribution Footprint

Many libraries and independent booksellers can discriminate against smaller publishers or independent listings through Amazon's CreateSpace. With our legal entity registering and distributing your title, you'll end up with access to markets you wouldn't otherwise see.

3

World-Class Production Quality

Our production staff are some of the best in the industry. They'll lovingly lay out your work like a professional, legitimate trade publication, and ensure compatibility across digital platforms for maximum title visibility and market penetration.
